Drawing Program

(A We.Publish coding problem)

enter command:
No canvas defined

Instructions

C w hCreate a new canvas of width w and height h.

L x1 y1 x2 y2Create a new line from (x1,y1) to (x2,y2).

R x1 y1 x2 y2Create a new rectangle, whose upper left corner is (x1,y1) and lower right corner is (x2,y2).

B x y cFill the entire area connected to (x,y) with "colour" c.

QQuit the program.
